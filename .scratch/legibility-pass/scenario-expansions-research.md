## Unsourced

None. All 16 rows (15 canonical scenarios) were sourced directly from each scenario's individual
Spirit Island Wiki page, each of which carries an explicit "Part of the ___ Expansion" /
"Part of the Base Game" statement.

## Reconciliation: 16 rows / 15 canonical scenarios

Confirmed. "Surges of Colonization" is a single scenario page on the wiki
(https://spiritislandwiki.com/index.php?title=Surges_of_Colonization) with two difficulty-modifier
variants documented on that same page: "Normal Surges" (base difficulty) and "Larger Surges" (the
higher-difficulty variant, which adds a Fear Card per Terror Level at setup and resolves extra
Explore cards on even turns). Both variants belong to the same scenario and the same expansion
(Nature Incarnate). The other 14 named scenarios each map one-to-one to one row. 14 + 2 = 16 rows,
14 + 1 = 15 canonical scenarios. Reconciles cleanly.
